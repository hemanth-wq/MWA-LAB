About this eBook

Information Technology (IT) is the use of tools and techniques to manage and process information. It's a broad field that includes:

  • Hardware and software: Computers, servers, routers, and other electronic devices that store, retrieve, transmit, and manipulate data.
  • Networking: Infrastructure that allows users to connect to the internet and communicate over networks.
  • Data science and mining: Extracting insights from large datasets by analyzing data with statistics, programming, and domain expertise.
  • Services: Supporting infrastructure that allows users to manage and deliver information using voice, data, and video.

IT is a foundation for digital infrastructure and plays a critical role in modern society, impacting communication, entertainment, and more. Some examples of IT in action include:

  • Building communications networks
  • Safeguarding data and information
  • Creating and administering databases
  • Helping employees troubleshoot problems with their devices

IT is a dynamic field that's constantly evolving as new technologies emerge and existing technologies advance.
